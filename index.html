About Me

I published my first app on the App Store in 2017, and since then I have been able to build several iOS and macOS applications for top brazilian and international companies. One of these apps has rewarded me a Scholarship at Apple WWDC 2018 for an environmental game called "Save the Trees!", which I developed while I was enrolled in Apple Developer Academy, a developer training program.

I graduated in Computer Engineering at State University of Campinas (UNICAMP), Brazil, and I have a Computer Science Master's Degree at the same university. In my Master's research I investigated how Machine Learning Algorithms could be used to improve Internet of Things (IoT) Systems, and I published a paper in the Brazilian Computing Society Congress about this.

All my work and educational experience have contributed to make me a professional capable of building different sort of computing solutions, with a focus on iOS development.
